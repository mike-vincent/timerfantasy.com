Privacy Policy

Last updated January 19, 2026

 

Overview

Timer Fantasy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device with no analytics, tracking, or advertising.

 

iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled, your timer settings sync across your devices using Apple's iCloud service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to it.

 

Analytics

Timer Fantasy does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising software.
